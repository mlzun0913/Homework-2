About Me

My name is Milton Zuniga and I have been a high school English teacher/university professor for 13 years. Throughout my career, I have served in various roles in the field of education. I have had the pleasure of teaching kindergarten to twelfth grade students in remedial and advanced classes. I have taught writing, literature, ESL and creative writing courses to undergraduate students. In addition, I have served in various leadership teams as well as an educational consultant for school improvement goals. Also, I have developed and supervised curricula across multiple disciplines and levels.

My education, BA, MA and MS degrees and are from American universities and most of my experience has been in classrooms in the US where I have a teaching license from the state of Florida. The last two years I have had the pleasure of working at the Institute of Applied Technology (IAT) in Abu Dhabi but now I am searching for opportunities for professional growth.
